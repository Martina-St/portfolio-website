MeetMe

The Project

MeetMe is an app that helps you to find locations where you can take your date out in Berlin. I came up with the idea for this app, designed the UI and conducted interviews and user tests.

Motivation

I wanted to create this app because “where to meet up with your date” is often discussed amongst my peers. After researching, I figured that no webpage or app focuses specifically on this issue.

Research

To get input from potential users, I conducted interviews. I wanted to figure out how my interviewees were planing their dates so far and how using MeetMe could bring additional value.

User Flow Diagram

Based on the interviews I determined the main functions and flows the app needed to have. I visualized them in the User Flow Diagram.

Wireframes

With the wireframes at hand, I went on to conduct user test and preference tests so I could implement the feedback in my final design.

Design Process 1

Looking back to my first design of the Home Page, I figure that I tried to put all the apps functions on one page. After feedback from my peers and researching, I structured my home page so that it looks much more like an actual landing page, still providing the user with all the necessary functions.

Design Process 2

After User Testing, I figured that one main issue was that users struggled with what to expect under each category. After a preference test, I went for “All”, “Drinks & Food” and “Activity”. Another significant change was the design of the cards. I figured that I needed to structure them in a way so that users get all the crucial information about the place at one glance.

Design Process 3

Within the design process, I concluded that filtering results and searching directly for a location are two different processes and therefore the functions for this don’t need to be packed on one page. So I split the original page into two. Therefore the navigation bar shows in the final version four icons!

Final Design

Within the final version, users can create their account and save their favourite places within their library. Users can browse through plenty of suggestions sorted by category and read more about every single location. The app also provides the option to filter the search, so users find exactly what they’re searching for.
