About

My Story

With an office management background, I understand the importance of straightforward practices. While working for startups I often was faced with implementing new processes. I always tried to answer the question: “How can we do this as efficient and easy to operate as possible?”. I approach my designs in the same way: I am a fan of applications with a straight forward flow that bring extra value to peoples lives. I strive for clean and neat design and I embrace the value of getting feedback from users.
